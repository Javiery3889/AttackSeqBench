   - **C: Resource Development:** This TTP involves developing the resources needed for the attack, which could include the malware itself.
   - **D: Execution:** This TTP involves running the malware, which happens after the malware has been obtained and prepared.

   - The most logical step before reusing the malware would be to collect or develop the malware.

   - D: Impact - This tactic involves actions that cause harm to the target system or data. It could be a result of the malware's activities but is not a direct step in the sequence described.

- The PE file was executed (B: Execution) to check the environment.
- The malware then used Process Hollowing (which is a technique within the Execution tactic) to inject into a process.
- The communication with the C2 server would likely be part of the Execution or Persistence tactics, but since it's a direct action to communicate, it's more closely aligned with Execution.

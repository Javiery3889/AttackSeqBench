- A: T1057-Process Discovery: This technique involves identifying running processes on a system. While it could be part of the malware's operation, it is not directly related to the collection and exfiltration of information.

- C: T1012-Query Registry: This technique involves querying the Windows registry, which is not relevant to iOS 7, as it is a Windows-specific technique.
- D: T1556.003-Pluggable Authentication Modules (PAM): This technique is used to collect information from a system, such as user credentials, which could be relevant to the collection of contact lists, text messages, and Wi-Fi status. It is also a technique that could be used to exfiltrate this information to a C2 server.

- Answer Choice C: T1572-Protocol Tunneling - This technique is used to bypass network defenses by tunneling traffic through another protocol. It could be used for continuous communication but is not as directly associated with the initial phishing delivery as T1071.
- Answer Choice D: T1036.004-Masquerade Task or Service - This technique involves impersonating a legitimate task or service to avoid detection. While it could be used in a broader attack, it is not specifically related to enabling continuous communication with CnC addresses.

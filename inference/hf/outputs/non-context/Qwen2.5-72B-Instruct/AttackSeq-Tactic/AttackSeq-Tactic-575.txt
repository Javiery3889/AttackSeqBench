   - **B: Lateral Movement**

   - **C: Discovery**
     - Discovery involves gathering information about the target system and network. This is usually done early in the attack to understand the environment and plan further actions.
   - **D: Command and Control**
     - Command and Control involves communicating with and controlling compromised systems. This is necessary for the attacker to manage the attack but is not directly related to the final destructive action of overwriting the MBR.

   - The wiper module overwriting the MBR is a destructive action that renders the system unusable. This aligns with the Impact tactic.

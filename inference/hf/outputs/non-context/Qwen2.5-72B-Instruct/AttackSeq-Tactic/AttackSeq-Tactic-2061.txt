     - Defense evasion involves techniques to avoid detection and analysis.

   - **Resource Development (D):**
     - Resource development involves preparing for an attack by gathering necessary resources.
     - The described actions are not related to resource development but to maintaining and ensuring the malware's presence on the system.

3. **Provide a Step-by-Step Reasoning and Final Answer:**

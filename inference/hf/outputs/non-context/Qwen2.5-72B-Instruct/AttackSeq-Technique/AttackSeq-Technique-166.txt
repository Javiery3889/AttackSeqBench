  - **Description**: This technique involves the development of new tools, capabilities, or tradecraft to support operations.

- **Answer Choice C: T1589-Gather Victim Identity Information**
  - **Description**: This technique involves collecting information about the identities of potential victims, such as names, email addresses, and other personal details.

- **Answer Choice D: T1594-Search Victim-Owned Websites**
  - **Description**: This technique involves searching for and identifying websites owned by the victim to gather information.

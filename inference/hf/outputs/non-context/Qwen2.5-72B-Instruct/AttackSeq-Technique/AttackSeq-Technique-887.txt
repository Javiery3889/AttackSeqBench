  - This technique involves exploiting vulnerabilities in remote services to gain access to a system. While this could be part of the initial access, the question specifically mentions the use of malicious URLs and exploit documents, which are more aligned with client-side exploitation.

- **D: T1203-Exploitation for Client Execution**
  - This technique involves exploiting client-side applications (e.g., web browsers, email clients) to execute malicious code. This aligns well with the use of malicious URLs and exploit documents to deliver malware.

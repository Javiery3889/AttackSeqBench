  - This technique involves gathering information about the victim's identity, such as usernames, email addresses, and other personal details. - **Initial Access**: The DNSpionage campaign likely used a method to gain initial access to the victim's system. Phishing (T1598) is a common and effective method for initial access, as it can trick users into providing credentials or clicking on malicious links.

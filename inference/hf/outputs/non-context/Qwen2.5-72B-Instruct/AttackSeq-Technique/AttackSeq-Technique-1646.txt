  - **Description**: This technique involves hiding files, registry keys, and processes to avoid detection by security tools and users.

  - **Description**: This technique involves manipulating process relationships to hide the presence of malicious processes.
  - **Evaluation**: While this technique can help hide processes, it is more specific to breaking process trees and may not cover hiding files and registry keys.

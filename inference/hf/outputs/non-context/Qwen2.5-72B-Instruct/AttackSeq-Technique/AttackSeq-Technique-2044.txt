  - **Description**: This technique involves the use of hardware to gather information, such as keyloggers or other physical devices.

- **Answer Choice D: T1566-Phishing**
  - **Description**: This technique involves sending fraudulent communications to trick individuals into revealing sensitive information or performing actions that benefit the attacker.

Phishing is a more direct and immediate action, whereas the initial phase is more about building trust and gathering information.

This is a direct match for the described action of downloading malware to facilitate the attack. After gaining access to additional accounts, the next logical step would be to transfer the necessary tools or malware to the compromised system.

- **C: T1104-Multi-Stage Channels**

- **D: T1098-Account Manipulation**
  - This technique involves manipulating existing accounts to maintain access or escalate privileges. While it is relevant to the context of accessing additional accounts, it does not directly address the downloading of malware.

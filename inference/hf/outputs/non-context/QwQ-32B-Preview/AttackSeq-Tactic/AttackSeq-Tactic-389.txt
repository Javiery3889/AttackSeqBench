Let's look at what each of these tactics means in the MITRE ATT&CK framework.

**Execution** is when an adversary runs malicious code or commands to carry out their objectives. This could be executing a payload after exploiting a vulnerability, for example.

**Impact** involves actions that affect the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of victim systems or data. This could include data deletion, encryption, or system shutdown.

**Command and Control** (C2) is the communication between the compromised system and the attacker's command server. This is how the attacker controls the malware and receives data from the infected system.

**Initial Access** is the first stage of an attack where the adversary tries to get a foothold in the target network. This could be through phishing emails, exploiting vulnerabilities, or other methods.

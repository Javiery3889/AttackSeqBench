**Command and Control (C2):** This is when attackers maintain communication with their malware on the target's system to control it and exfiltrate data. It's like the attackers' way of managing their operations on the compromised system.

**Privilege Escalation:** This is when attackers try to gain higher levels of access or permissions on a system, often to perform more sophisticated actions or to move laterally within a network.

**Discovery:** This tactic involves attackers gathering information about the system and network to understand their environment better, which helps them plan their next steps.

**Credential Access:** This is when attackers try to obtain credentials (like usernames and passwords) from the system to use for further attacks or to maintain persistent access.

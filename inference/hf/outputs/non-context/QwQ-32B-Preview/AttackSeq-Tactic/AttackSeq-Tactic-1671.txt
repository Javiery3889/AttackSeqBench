**Impact (D)**: This tactic refers to the actions adversaries take to achieve their objectives, such as data destruction, theft, or disruption. Again, this seems to occur after access has been gained, not before.

1. **Reconnaissance**: Research and gather information about the target.

3. **Execution**: Run malicious code on the target's systems.

4. **Persistence**: Ensure continued access to the target's network.

11. **Command and Control**: Communicate with compromised systems.

12. **Exfiltration**: Transfer stolen data out of the target's network.

13. **Impact**: Achieve the adversary's objective, such as data destruction or manipulation.

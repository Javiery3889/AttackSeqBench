- **Defense Evasion**: This tactic involves techniques used by the attacker to prevent detection or avoid security controls.

- **Credential Access**: This is about obtaining credentials from the target system, which can be used for lateral movement or other activities.

- **Privilege Escalation**: This involves the attacker trying to gain higher levels of access or permissions on the system.

**D: Privilege Escalation**

- Privilege escalation involves gaining higher levels of access or permissions. While obtaining credentials could lead to privilege escalation, the act of accessing registry keys for stored passwords is more directly related to credential access.

- **Credential Access**: As the name suggests, this tactic involves actions aimed at stealing account names and passwords, API keys, or other types of credentials.

- **Initial Access**: This is about how an adversary gains their first foothold into a network or system. This could be through phishing emails, exploiting vulnerabilities, or other methods.

**Command and Control (C2):** This tactic involves the methods threat actors use to communicate with their malware on the target system, often to receive commands or exfiltrate data.

**Impact:** This tactic includes actions that result in the disruption, degradation, or destruction of systems, data, or services, such as data deletion, system shutdown, or other destructive activities.

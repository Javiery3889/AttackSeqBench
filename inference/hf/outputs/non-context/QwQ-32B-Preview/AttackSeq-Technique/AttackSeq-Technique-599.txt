This seems plausible, but let's look at the other options to see if there's a better fit.

This appears to be a strong candidate.

T1059 - Command and Scripting Interpreter refers to adversaries using command shells or script interpreters to execute commands and scripts. This is a very common technique used in many stages of an attack.

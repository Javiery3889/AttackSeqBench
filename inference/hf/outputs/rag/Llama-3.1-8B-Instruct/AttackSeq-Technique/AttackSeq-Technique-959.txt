Analysis of the Question:

- Tactic: Initial Access, Technique: T1566.001-Spearphishing Attachment: This further specifies that the phishing was through a spear phishing attachment.

Comparing and Evaluating Answer Choices:

- B: T1204-User Execution: This technique involves an attacker convincing a user to execute a file or process, which is exactly what happened in this scenario. The macro was executed by the user, and then it downloaded and executed malicious files.

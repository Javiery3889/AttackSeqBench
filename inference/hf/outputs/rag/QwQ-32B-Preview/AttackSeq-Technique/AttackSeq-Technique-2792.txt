A web service is a method of communication between two electronic devices over the World Wide Web. More specifically, it's a service that is available over HTTP and can be accessed by other applications.

Software Discovery involves an adversary trying to get information about the software installed on a system. This could be to identify vulnerabilities or to understand the environment better.

- Tactic: Persistence, Technique: T1543.003-Windows Service: This suggests that the malware is attempting to persist on the system, which is relevant to the question as it mentions the registration of a service.

Based on the analysis of the question and the related TTPs, the most plausible sequence of TTPs after the installation process involving the registration of a second service pointing to the backdoor DLL is the addition of a system service to persist on the system.

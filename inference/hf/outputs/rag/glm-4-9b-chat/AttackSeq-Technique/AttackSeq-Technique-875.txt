- This technique involves hijacking the Secure Input Protocol (SIP) or Trust Provider to bypass security controls. This is a method that could be used to execute the malware without detection, which aligns with the use of DLL side-loading for Defense Evasion.

- The initial access via email delivery of Gh0st RAT suggests a delivery method that could be followed by Defense Evasion techniques.

These TTPs are related to persistence mechanisms, which is the act of ensuring that a malicious actor's presence is maintained on a system after initial access. The techniques mentioned are methods that malware can use to persist on a system, such as creating registry run keys or adding entries to the startup folder.

Step 3: Compare and Evaluate

Pass the Ticket is a technique used for lateral movement within a network, not persistence. This technique is also not directly related to the persistence mechanisms mentioned.

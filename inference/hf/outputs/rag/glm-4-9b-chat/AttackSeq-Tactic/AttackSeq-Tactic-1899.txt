These TTPs suggest that the attack involves:
- The execution of a malicious file (T1204.002)

C: Reconnaissance

D: Execution
- Execution (T1059) is the tactic that includes the techniques T1059.007 (JavaScript) and T1204.002 (Malicious File). The sequence described in the question involves the delivery of malicious JavaScript files, which is a form of execution. Additionally, the malware gpsvc.exe accepting command line arguments is also an execution activity. This TTP is the most directly related to the sequence of events described in the question.

C: Command and Control
- Command and Control (C2) is the process of maintaining communication between the attacker and the compromised system. While this is a critical part of many attacks, the question is focused on the search for documents, not the communication aspect.

D: Credential Access
- Credential Access is the act of obtaining credentials from a system. This is not directly related to the search for documents, although it could be a subsequent step in the attack.

The TTPs are categorized under the following ATT&CK tactics:

- Initial Access: This tactic involves the initial exploitation of a vulnerability to gain a beachhead on a system.
- Spearphishing Link: This is a technique that uses a malicious link to deliver malware or to initiate an attack.

Step 3: Compare and Evaluate

- This tactic involves moving laterally within a network after initial access. The scenario does not describe any movement within a network or system, so this tactic is not applicable.

D: Reconnaissance
- This tactic involves gathering information about a target. While the decoy documents could be seen as a form of reconnaissance, the question specifically asks about the tactic that occurred after the decoy documents and malicious links were used. Reconnaissance would have likely occurred before these steps.

Based on the analysis, the scenario described in the question aligns best with the Execution tactic, as it involves directing victims to a malicious site, which is a direct action that leads to the execution of malicious code.

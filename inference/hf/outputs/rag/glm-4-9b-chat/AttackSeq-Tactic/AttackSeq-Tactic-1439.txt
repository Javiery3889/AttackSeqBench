This is a plausible sequence because after obtaining credentials, the attacker would likely use them to gain further access or control over the system, which could lead to the redirection to a YouTube clip.

While discovery is a critical step in an attack, it is not as directly related to the sequence of events described in the question, which focuses on the collection of credentials and the redirection to a YouTube clip.

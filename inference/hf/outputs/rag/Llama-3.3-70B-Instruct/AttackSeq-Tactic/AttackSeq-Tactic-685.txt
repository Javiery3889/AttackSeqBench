   - **Lateral Movement (C)**: This involves moving from one system to another within the network, which could be a precursor to spreading the malware further, especially if the malware is looking to infect other systems via USB.
   - **Defense Evasion (D)**: This tactic is about evading detection, which is crucial for the malware's survival but not directly related to the spreading action described.


Step 1: Analyze the CTI Outline
- Initial Access: The Sednit espionage group uses CVE-2015-5119 Flash exploit to target Eastern European institutions.
- The attack begins with a spear-phishing email with a URL mimicking a legitimate domain.
- The victim's browser is directed to a landing page that collects system information.
- If the system meets criteria, the Flash exploit "flash_video_x86.swf" is delivered.
- Persistence: If the Flash exploitation is successful, a first-stage backdoor malware is received.
- The malware includes the Hacking Team's Windows privilege escalation exploit.
- The malware establishes persistence on the machine through a scheduled task that runs with the highest privileges.

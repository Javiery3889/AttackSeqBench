- Communication with C&C infrastructure after WMI persistence

- The CTI outline states that the malware communicated with command-and-control (C&C) servers via HTTP over port 80.

- The answer choice A: T1071.001-Web Protocols is the most specific and accurate TTP that matches the communication method described in the CTI outline.

Final Answer: A: T1071.001-Web Protocols

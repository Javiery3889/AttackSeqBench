- **Collection**: The adversary gathers information or data of interest.

- **Command and Control (C2)**: The adversary communicates with compromised systems.

- **Exfiltration**: The adversary transfers stolen data out of the network.

- **Impact**: The adversary causes disruption, such as data deletion or system damage.

Let's look at the answer choices:

C: **Persistence** - This tactic involves techniques that threat actors use to maintain their access to a system, even through system reboots or other interruptions. This aligns perfectly with the creation of scheduled tasks to ensure the malware continues to run.

D: **Command and Control** - This involves the communication between the malware and the threat actor's command and control servers. While essential for controlling the malware, it doesn't specifically address the mechanism for maintaining access beyond a reboot.

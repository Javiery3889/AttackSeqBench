In many attack sequences, the pattern is:

1. Gain initial access.

2. Establish persistence.

5. Perform lateral movement to access more systems.

8. Exfiltrate the data.

Given this typical sequence, after Discovery and before Collection, Lateral Movement is a likely tactic.

**C: T1558.002 - Silver Ticket**

This is a Kerberos ticketing attack that allows attackers to access services without authenticating to the domain controller. It's more related to credential access and lateral movement, which aren't directly mentioned in the outline yet.

**D: T1071 - Application Layer Protocol**

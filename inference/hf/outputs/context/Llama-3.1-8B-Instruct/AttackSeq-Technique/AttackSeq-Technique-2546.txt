6. **Defense Evasion**: Deep Panda used a novel kernel rootkit, Fire Chili, to hide and protect malicious artifacts.
7. **Command and Control**: The Milestone backdoor communicated with a C2 server uncompressed.
8. **Exfiltration**: The Milestone backdoor exfiltrated data over the C2 channel.

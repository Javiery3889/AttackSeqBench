### Step-by-Step Analysis

#### 1. Analyze the CTI Report

**Key Details:**
- **Defense Evasion:**
  - CosmicStrand is a UEFI firmware rootkit.
  - It modifies firmware images of Gigabyte or ASUS motherboards with the H81 chipset.
  - The rootkit is introduced into the CSMCORE DXE driver, redirecting the entry point to attacker-supplied code in the .reloc section.
  - This leads to the download and deployment of a malicious component inside Windows.
  - CosmicStrand attempts to disable PatchGuard to avoid detection.
- **Persistence:**
  - Sets up hooks in the boot manager and Windows kernel loader to deploy a kernel-level implant into Windows systems at boot.
- **Command and Control (C2):**
  - Uses user-mode components or other undiscovered variants and C2 servers.
  - Two C2 servers have been identified.
  - Activity periods indicate intermittent exploitation of victim machines.

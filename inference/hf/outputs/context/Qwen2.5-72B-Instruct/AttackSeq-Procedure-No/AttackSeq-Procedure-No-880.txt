#### 3. Compare and Evaluate

**Key Points from the CTI Report:**

- The group targets national government entities and expands footholds by attacking from one compromised entity to another.
- The group uses infected ministries as C&C servers to collect, relay, and route stolen data.

**Evaluation of Answer Choices:**
- **A: Yes**

  - The group's methods for lateral movement and target identification are not detailed in the report.

- **B: No**
  - The report does not provide any evidence that the Naikon APT group uses AWS Systems Manager Inventory.
  - The group's methods for lateral movement and target identification are more likely to involve the capabilities already listed, such as file/directory manipulation, process enumeration, and OS information gathering.

#### 4. Provide a Step-by-Step Reasoning and Final Answer

**Reasoning:**

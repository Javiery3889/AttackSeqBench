- Data was encrypted with a public key, requiring a private key to decrypt.

- The application supported different malware campaigns.

**Exfiltration:**

- Data was encrypted and archived, preventing unauthorized access.

**Others:**

- The malware targeted the Middle East and was modular in design.
- The attackers employed data security compartmentalization techniques.

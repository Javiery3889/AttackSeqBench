#### 1. Analyze the CTI Report

**Initial Access:**

- Extension can search, archive, read, label, mark as spam, delete, refresh, forward emails, and perform function searches in Gmail.
- Access user data for all websites, display notifications, and modify privacy settings in Firefox.

**Others:**

- TA413 has used the Scanbox framework since at least June 2020.
- Linked to watering hole attacks targeting Tibetan organizations in 2019.
- Campaign leverages the legitimate domain Tibet[.]net as a decoy.
- Delivers Scanbox via the malicious browser extension.
- TA413 has used COVID-19 and self-immolation themed lures to deliver Sepulcher malware via malicious RTF files.
- Despite public disclosures, TA413 has not made significant operational changes and is expected to continue targeting the Tibetan Diaspora.

### Step-by-Step Analysis

#### 1. Analyze the CTI Report

**Reconnaissance:**
- The campaign used job recruitment techniques to gather information.
- Targets were defense and government-related organizations.

**Initial Access:**
- Malicious documents with a macro were distributed.
- The macro injected the Sharpshooter downloader into the memory of Word.
- The downloader retrieved the Rising Sun implant from a control server.

**Execution:**
- The shellcode executed by the macro in winword.exe acted as a downloader.
- It resolved Libraries and APIs, downloaded the second-stage payload and a decoy document, and executed both payloads.

**Persistence:**
- The Rising Sun implant ensured persistence by downloading the second-stage binary to the startup folder.

**Discovery:**
- The Rising Sun implant performed initial reconnaissance by fetching network adapter info, computer name, user name, IP address information, native system information, and OS product name from the registry.
- It decrypted additional information during the reconnaissance process.

**Exfiltration:**
- The Rising Sun implant encrypted data using RC4 and obfuscated it with Base64 before exfiltrating it to the control server via HTTP POST requests.

- This reconnaissance activity is part of the implant's functionality and occurs before or during the exfiltration process.

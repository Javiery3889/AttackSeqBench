### Step-by-Step Analysis

#### 1. Analyze the CTI Report

**Initial Access:**
- The iOS malware (XAgent) is installed on already compromised systems.
- It is similar to the SEDNIT malware for Windows.

**Execution:**
- The XAgent app runs in the background on iOS 7 and restarts immediately if terminated.
- On iOS 8, the app's icon is visible and it cannot restart automatically.

**Discovery:**
- The app collects various types of information:
  - Text messages
  - Contact lists
  - Pictures
  - Geo-location data
  - Voice recordings
  - List of installed apps
  - List of processes
  - Wi-Fi status

#### 2. Analyze the Question

The question asks about the ATT&CK technique that most likely facilitates uploading files via FTP to the C2 server after the XAgent app collects information.

#### 3. Compare and Evaluate

**Answer Choices:**

D: **T1105-Ingress Tool Transfer**
- This technique involves transferring tools or other files from an external system into the compromised environment. While it is about transferring files, it is typically used for bringing in additional tools, not for uploading collected data.

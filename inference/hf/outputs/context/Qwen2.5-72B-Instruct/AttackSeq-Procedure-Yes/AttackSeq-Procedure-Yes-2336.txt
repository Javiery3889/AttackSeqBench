### Step-by-Step Analysis

#### 1. Analyze the CTI Report

**Initial Access:**
- The attack starts with a phishing email impersonating a senior partner of the Brazilian law firm 'Veirano Advogados'.
- The email contains a PowerPoint add-in document with a '.ppa' extension.
- The document includes an auto-open VBA macro designed to execute further malicious activities.

**Persistence:**
- The malware establishes persistence by creating a scheduled task to run the 'mshta.exe' utility with the URL 'hxxps://pocasideiascdt.blogspot[.]com/' every two hours.

**Command and Control:**
- The 'RevengeRAT' payload, once executed, establishes communication with its command and control servers.
- The C2 servers are 'office365update[.]duckdns.org' and 'systen32.ddns[.]net'.

**Others:**
- The 'Document.exe' file, with a Pokemon Megaball image as its icon, is hosted at 'cdtmaster.com[.]br' and downloaded by the 'Z3j.vbs' script.
- The 'Outlook.exe' payload opens a listening TCP socket on localhost.
- The domain 'cdtmaster.com[.]br' also hosted other suspicious files, including a variant of 'njRAT'.

#### 2. Analyze the Question

The question asks whether it is likely that the VBA macro will execute commands using 'mshta.exe' to download and execute a dropper from a Blogspot URL before the malware establishes persistence by creating a scheduled task to execute 'mshta.exe' with a specific URL every two hours.

### Step-by-Step Analysis

#### 1. Analyze the CTI Report

**Initial Access:**
- Phishing emails with links to download Office documents.
- Office Macro Scripts are used to load the Chinotto malware.

**Execution:**
- Office Macro Scripts load Chinotto malware.
- PowerShell and Golang malware are used.
- BITSAdmin for DLL Side-Loading to execute malicious scripts and establish command and control.

**Defense Evasion:**
- DLL Side-Loading techniques are used.
- evc.dll and mfc42u.dll are used to load and execute the Chinotto malware without detection.

**Command and Control:**
- HTTP requests to specific PHP scripts on C2 servers.
- Golang malware communicates with the attacker's channel using Ably key and Client Hello messages.

**Exfiltration:**
- Data is sent to the attackers' infrastructure using specific PHP scripts.
- BITSAdmin is used to transfer malware to the victims' systems.

**Others:**
- 'Defend Forward' approach by the US Department of Defense.
- Incident response process includes malware analysis, C2 monitoring, threat tracking, and countermeasures.

### Step-by-Step Analysis

#### 1. Analyze the CTI Report

**Initial Access:**

- The email uses social engineering in Spanish to trick users into enabling macros.

**Execution:**
- The malicious macro, once enabled, downloads a second-stage downloader.
- The second-stage downloader fetches an encrypted third-stage payload, which includes the Lokibot malware.
- The infection chain involves multiple stages and layers of encryption to execute Lokibot on the victim's machine.

**Defense Evasion:**
- The Lokibot campaign uses process hollowing and code injection into a suspended process to bypass User Account Control (UAC).

**Others:**
- The campaign uses a complex, multi-stage, multi-layered dropper.
- The adversaries are capable of stealing multiple types of credentials and sensitive information.
- The infection chain is sophisticated, with three stages and three layers of encryption.
- The dropper injects code into a suspended process to bypass UAC and uses process hollowing to execute its final payload.

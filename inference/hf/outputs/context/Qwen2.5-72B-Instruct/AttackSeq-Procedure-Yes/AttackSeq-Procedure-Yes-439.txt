### Step-by-Step Analysis

#### 1. Analyze the CTI Report

**Initial Access:**
- The Sednit group uses email attachments with malicious documents or archives.
- These documents contain VBA macros to download and run the first stage payload.
- The Delphi downloader is often the first stage, displaying a splash window with a bogus error message.
- The AutoIt downloader can be the first or second stage, also displaying a splash window to mislead the victim.

**Execution:**
- The Delphi downloader creates a process using `cmd.exe /c SYSTEMINFO & TASKLIST` to gather information.
- It sends the gathered information to a C&C server via an HTTP POST request.
- The AutoIt downloader, when acting as the first stage, executes the payload received from the C&C server.
- The Delphi backdoor, as the final stage, uses `SetTimer` to execute callback functions.

**Persistence:**
- The Delphi downloader adds a Windows registry entry for persistence.
- The AutoIt downloader uses a similar persistence mechanism.

**Defense Evasion:**
- The Delphi downloader displays a splash window with a bogus error message.
- The AutoIt downloader shows a splash screen related to the binary icon.
- The Delphi backdoor uses AES encryption for configuration data and C&C communications.

**Discovery:**
- The AutoIt downloader performs extensive reconnaissance.
- It detects sandbox and virtual environments.
- It retrieves a list of installed software, determines the Windows version, and gathers process lists, hard drive information, and screenshots.
- It uses WMI objects to collect various information about the victim computer.

**Command and Control:**
- The Delphi backdoor communicates with the C&C server by storing reports in temporary files.
- These files are stored in hidden directories and encrypted with AES-256-ECB before sending.

**Others:**
- The Sednit group has been active since 2004.
- They target entities such as embassies, ministries of foreign affairs, and diplomats.
- Zebrocy is a family of malware used by the group, including downloaders and backdoors written in Delphi and AutoIt.
- Zebrocy campaigns use malicious documents with VBA macros to download the first stage payload.

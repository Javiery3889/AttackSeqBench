#### 1. Analyze the CTI Report

**Reconnaissance:**
- FIN4 targets individuals with access to confidential M&A and market-moving information, especially in the healthcare and pharmaceutical industries.
- They use spearphishing emails written by native English speakers familiar with investment terminology and the inner workings of public companies.

**Initial Access:**
- FIN4 employs spearphishing emails to gain initial access.

- They also use fake Outlook Web App (OWA) login pages to capture credentials.

**Defense Evasion:**
- FIN4 creates rules in victims' Microsoft Outlook accounts to automatically delete emails containing words like 'hacked', 'phish', 'malware', etc.

**Credential Access:**

- They also use fake OWA login pages to capture user credentials.

**Command and Control:**

#### 2. Analyze the Question

**Question:**
- After conducting reconnaissance by targeting individuals with access to confidential M&A and market-moving information, which ATT&CK tactic most likely occurred before FIN4 gained initial access through spearphishing emails with embedded VBA macros?

**Answer Choices:**
- A: Reconnaissance
- B: Persistence

#### 3. Compare and Evaluate

**Reconnaissance:**
- This is the initial phase where FIN4 identifies and targets individuals with access to confidential information.

**Initial Access:**
- This is the phase where FIN4 gains access to the target's system, typically through spearphishing emails with embedded VBA macros.

**Defense Evasion:**
- This occurs after initial access, where FIN4 creates rules to delete suspicious emails.

**Persistence:**
- This involves maintaining long-term access to the target's system, which is not mentioned as occurring before initial access.

**Resource Development:**
- This involves preparing the necessary resources (e.g., creating phishing emails, setting up C2 infrastructure) before the attack. This is a plausible step that occurs before initial access.

#### 4. Provide a Step-by-Step Reasoning and Final Answer

1. **Reconnaissance** is the first step where FIN4 identifies and targets individuals with access to confidential information.
2. **Resource Development** is the next logical step where FIN4 prepares the necessary resources, such as creating spearphishing emails and setting up C2 infrastructure.
3. **Initial Access** is the phase where FIN4 uses the prepared resources to gain access to the target's system through spearphishing emails with embedded VBA macros.

Given the sequence of events, the most plausible tactic that occurred before FIN4 gained initial access is **Resource Development**.

**Final Answer: D: Resource Development**

**Lateral Movement:**

**Reconnaissance:**
- This tactic involves gathering information to plan future actions. The report does not indicate that reconnaissance occurs after the initial access and before persistence.

**Persistence:**
- This tactic involves maintaining access to a victim's system. The report states that macro-enabled template files maintain persistence by replacing the victim's default Microsoft Word template. This occurs after the initial access.

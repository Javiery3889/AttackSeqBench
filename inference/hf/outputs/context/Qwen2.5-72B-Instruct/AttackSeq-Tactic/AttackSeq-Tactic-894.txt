- **Defense Evasion**: They use anti-forensics techniques to prevent clues from being left on disk or in the registry.
- **Discovery**: POWERSHOWER collects system information and sends it to the C2 server.
- **Command and Control**: POWERSHOWER communicates with the C2 server, sending system information and receiving instructions.

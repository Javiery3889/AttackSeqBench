### Step-by-Step Analysis

#### 1. Analyze the CTI Report

**Initial Access:**
- GroundPeony used spear-phishing emails with lure documents and URL obfuscation to download and execute malicious payloads from compromised websites.
- This established communication with a C&C server.

**Defense Evasion:**
- The group used DLL side-loading with "version.dll" to decode and launch shellcode from "mic.doc".
- The decoding process involved custom XOR operations.
- "mic.doc" had its MZ header removed and was responsible for downloading and decoding encoded shellcode.

**Command and Control:**
- Communication with the C&C server was established through the domain "app.onedrivo.com" with the IP address 172.93.189.239.
- The "mic.ver" config file contained encoded information for connecting to the C&C server.

**Others:**
- Contextual information about the threat group, including its targets and infrastructure.

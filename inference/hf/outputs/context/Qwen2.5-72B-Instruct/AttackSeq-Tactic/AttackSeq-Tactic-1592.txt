#### 2. Analyze the Question

  - B: Execution

#### 4. Reasoning and Final Answer
- **Lateral Movement (A)**: This tactic involves moving through a network to expand access. The report does not mention any lateral movement activities.
- **Execution (B)**: This tactic involves running malicious code. The initial execution of Industroyer2 and the deployment of wipers are part of the execution phase, but the question asks what happens after the initial execution.
- **Impact (C)**: This tactic involves manipulating, interrupting, or destroying systems and data. The deployment of wipers like CaddyWiper and SoloShred to target systems and cause damage aligns with the impact phase.

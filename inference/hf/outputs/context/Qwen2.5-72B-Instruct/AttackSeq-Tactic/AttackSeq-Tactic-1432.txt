#### 2. Analyze the Question

The question asks which ATT&CK tactic most likely occurred after StellarParticle used AdFind and custom tooling to identify pieces of information from Active Directory and systems while preparing to interrogate other infrastructure for additional access pathways.

#### 3. Compare and Evaluate

**AdFind and Custom Tooling:**
- AdFind is a tool used to query Active Directory for information.
- Custom tooling to identify pieces of information from systems suggests the threat actor is gathering information about the network and systems.

**Preparation for Interrogation:**
- The threat actor is preparing to interrogate other infrastructure for additional access pathways, which indicates they are looking for more information to move laterally or gain deeper access.

**Relevant ATT&CK Tactics:**
- **Reconnaissance:** Gathering information to plan future actions.
- **Discovery:** Attempting to understand the environment to identify potential targets and access pathways.
- **Credential Access:** Obtaining credentials to gain access to additional systems.
- **Impact:** Causing disruption or damage to the victim's systems.

**Evaluation:**
- **Reconnaissance** involves gathering information to plan future actions, but the report does not specifically mention this as a next step after using AdFind.
- **Discovery** involves understanding the environment to identify potential targets and access pathways, which aligns with the use of AdFind and custom tooling to gather information.
- **Credential Access** involves obtaining credentials, which could be a next step but is not the immediate focus after using AdFind.
- **Impact** involves causing disruption or damage, which is not relevant to the information gathering phase.

#### 4. Provide a Step-by-Step Reasoning and Final Answer

**Reasoning:**
- The use of AdFind and custom tooling to identify pieces of information from Active Directory and systems is a clear indication of the threat actor's efforts to understand the environment.
- This aligns with the **Discovery** tactic, which involves gathering information to identify potential targets and access pathways.

**Final Answer:**
D: Discovery

- **Discovery**: After gaining initial access, attackers often perform discovery to understand the network environment, identify valuable assets, and plan further actions.
- **Exfiltration**: This tactic involves stealing data from the target network, which typically occurs after the attackers have established a foothold and identified valuable data.
- **Defense Evasion**: This tactic involves techniques to avoid detection and analysis, which can occur at various stages of the attack but is often used to maintain persistence and avoid being caught.

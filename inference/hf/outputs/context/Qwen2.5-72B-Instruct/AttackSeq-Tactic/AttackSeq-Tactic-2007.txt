- **Exfiltration**: This is the final step where data is sent out from the victim's system.

To determine which tactic occurred before the exfiltration, we need to consider the logical sequence of events in a typical cyber attack:
1. **Initial Access**: The attackers gain entry into the target system.
2. **Execution**: The malware is deployed.
3. **Defense Evasion**: The malware maintains its presence without detection.
4. **Discovery**: The attackers gather information about the victim's system.
5. **Command and Control**: The attackers establish a communication channel to control the malware.
6. **Exfiltration**: The attackers send the gathered data out of the victim's system.

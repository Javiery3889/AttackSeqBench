#### 3. Compare and Evaluate
- **Resource Development**: This tactic involves establishing and maintaining the infrastructure and resources needed for an attack. It typically occurs before the attack is launched.
- **Defense Evasion**: This tactic involves techniques to avoid detection and analysis. The report explicitly mentions that the attackers released a new version of the malware to evade detection, which aligns with this tactic.
- **Reconnaissance**: This tactic involves gathering information to support future operations. It typically occurs before the attack is launched.
- **Command and Control**: This tactic involves techniques to communicate with systems under the attacker's control. While the attackers moved their C&C domains to new IP addresses, this is more of a defensive measure to maintain control rather than a new tactic.

#### 1. Analyze the CTI Report

**Reconnaissance:**
- The campaign used job recruitment activity to gather information.
- Targets were defense and government-related organizations.

**Initial Access:**
- Malicious documents with a macro injected the Sharpshooter downloader into Word's memory.
- The documents were distributed via an IP address in the United States and through Dropbox.

**Persistence:**
- The Rising Sun implant downloaded the second-stage binary to the startup folder to ensure persistence.

**Discovery:**
- The Rising Sun implant performed initial reconnaissance by fetching various system and network information.

**Command and Control:**
- The implant communicated with control servers using HTTP POST requests with encrypted and obfuscated data.

**Exfiltration:**
- The implant exfiltrated data using RC4 encryption and Base64 obfuscation via HTTP POST requests.

**Others:**

- The implant shares similarities with Lazarus Group attacks but may include false flags.
